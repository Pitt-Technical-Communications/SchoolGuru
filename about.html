Use SchoolGuru to stay on top of your study periods





About

The SchoolGuru website can help you figure out when to study for the many assignments that they have planned over the next week.
You can enter all the assignment information in "SchoolGuru" main website to input assignments, tests, readings, ect.
With the information you gave to us about the tasks, the site will prioritize when you should work on each task throughout the week.
You can then export the week view of work by clicking the "Enter" buttom, or "Export" at the masthead.
Note: You must put in all known assignments, as you cannot recall data from a previous session on this site.




Frequently Asked Questions


Q1. How can SchoolGuru improve my studying efficiency?


A: SchoolGuru divides tasks into smaller parts and schedules time to work on them throughout the week. This helps keep you on track and work on bite-size pieces throughout the week instead of trying to cram last minute.


Q2. How many assignments can I add to the SchoolGuru planner?


A: You can add as many as you want, but you need to add them one at a time.


Q3. How can I use SchoolGuru efficiently?


A: Input your assignments for the next couple of weeks. Assignments due a long time from now will not show up in the current week's planner.


Q4. What should I know before I use SchoolGuru?


A: SchoolGuru utilizes all input fields to organize your study sessions. Make sure to fill in all input fields before adding assignments.


Q5. What should I do if I want to delete an assignment?


A: You cannot delete assignments, so you must leave or refresh the site. However, this will delete all previous assignments as well.


Q6. Can I get email reminders?


A: That feature is unavailable at this time. However, it may be available in a future version of this tool.
